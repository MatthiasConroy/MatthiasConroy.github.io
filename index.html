Ok, how do I use this?

Just click on the name of whichever album you like more! If you love or hate both albums equally, or simply don't know either of the albums, click "I feel the same way about both".

Using this sorter can take between 127 and 448 "battles", so please make yourself comfortable and take care of any responsibilities prior to starting your sorting.
